Our Story

Serving Healthcare Communities in Ghana and beyond.

NTLF Medical was established with a clear and urgent mission: to make high-quality medical and laboratory supplies accessible and affordable to hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, and laboratories across Ghana and beyond.

Located at Broadcasting Junction, Off the Accra-Winneba Road, we are strategically positioned to serve healthcare facilities across Accra and beyond. Our team combines deep product knowledge with a genuine commitment to exceptional service.

From single-use consumables to advanced medical equipment, we stock over 67 certified products at competitive GH₵ prices. We understand the critical importance of reliable supply chains in healthcare — when supplies run out, patients are at risk. That's why dependability is at the core of everything we do.
